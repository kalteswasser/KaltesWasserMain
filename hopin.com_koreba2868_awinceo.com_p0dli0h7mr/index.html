Why Hopin?

Make distance irrelevant

Whether you’re bringing your physical event online or building from the ground up, Hopin helps you create exciting events for your global audience. No travel required.

Host full-scale events for millions

Hopin is built for events of all shapes and sizes—from small gatherings of a few dozen to massive conferences with hundreds of thousands of people. It doesn't matter how big or small. Every Hopin event has live video chat and ways to meet people.

Host public or private events

Hopin is secure. Organizers can make their events private and password-protected, or hidden and invite-only. You can also sell tickets or make your event free, or a mix of both. Hopin is flexible.

Apply your branding and style perfectly

Hopin lets you to change the colors of the event interior to your branded colors so your brand will be recognized at a glance.

Benefits of hosting your events on Hopin

You get more time to focus on connecting with people. There’s less travel stress. More customization and flexibility. Easier speaker scheduling. Plenty of sponsorship opportunities and deeper data analytics.
